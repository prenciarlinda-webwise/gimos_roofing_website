Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Last Updated: January 9, 2026

Introduction

Gimo's Renovation & Roofing ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website gimosroofing.com or use our services.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Request a free estimate through our website or third-party tools (such as Roofr)
  • Contact us via phone, email, or WhatsApp
  • Fill out forms on our website
  • Apply for financing through our partners (such as EnerBank)

This information may include:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Property address
  • Project details and preferences

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type
  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Referring website

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide estimates and respond to your inquiries
  • Schedule and perform roofing services
  • Communicate with you about your project
  • Improve our website and services
  • Send promotional communications (with your consent)
  • Comply with legal obligations

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google Analytics: To analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Google Ads: To measure advertising effectiveness
  • Roofr: To provide instant roofing estimates
  • EnerBank: To process financing applications
  • WhatsApp: For customer communication
  • Cloudflare: For website security and performance

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing the use of your information.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our website and improve your experience. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or indicate when a cookie is being sent.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
