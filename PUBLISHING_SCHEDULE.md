## Content Requirements for 90%+ Unique

Each page must have unique:

1. **Hero section** - Unique tagline and description
2. **Intro paragraphs** - Area-specific history, demographics, housing stock
3. **Local Evidence section** - Specific landmarks, streets, neighborhoods, challenges
4. **Services section** - Tailored service descriptions for the area
5. **Why Choose Us** - Area-specific reasons
6. **Materials section** - Pricing and recommendations based on local homes
7. **Climate section** - Area-specific weather challenges
8. **FAQs** - Unique questions relevant to that area
9. **Neighborhoods list** - Comprehensive local neighborhoods
